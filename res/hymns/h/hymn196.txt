["1 Lord, the ancient types and symbols,\nAs our all Thyself portrayed;\nAs was shadowed in those figures\nReal to us Thou now art made.\nContemplating such a picture\nAs we on its wonders gaze\nHow we marvel at Thy riches,\nAnd our song of worship raise.", "2 Lord, Thou art our true Passover\nGod passed over us thru Thee;\nBy Thyself and Thy redemption,\nWe with God have harmony.\nThou, the Lamb of God, redeemedst us,\nWith Thyself and with Thy blood;\nWe apply Thy blood, our ransom\nEating Thee, our real food.", "3 Lord, Thou art the Bread from heaven\nThe unleavened Bread of life;\nEating Thee, with Thee we mingle\nCeasing from our sin and strife.\nLamb and Bread are both Thy figures\nShowing Thou art life to us;\nFeasting on Thee at Thy table\nWe enjoy Thy riches thus.", "4 Lord, Thou art the Heav'nly Manna\nAs our daily food supply;\nStrengthening and energizing\nAll our need to satisfy.\nLiving Rock Thou also art, Lord\nCleft for us with life to flow;\nDrinking of this living water\nThirst is quenched, Thy life we know.", "5 Lord, Thou art the Land of Canaan-\nElevated, rich and good\nFlowing with both milk and honey,\nIn a glorious plenitude.\nBy Thy surplus God we worship\nIn Thy fellowship we move;\nThus in love we're joined together,\nAnd God's building we will prove."]
